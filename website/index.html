The Origin of Peter Parker


Before being Spider-Man, the hero was just Peter Parker. As a baby, he was orphaned after his parents (CIA agents) were executed. In this way, Peter ended up living with his uncles Ben and May Parker. From a young age, he was interested in scientific and technological matters, being very intelligent. On the other hand, he was also extremely shy and bullied at school. Things changed when, during a demonstration on handling nuclear waste, he was bitten by a spider irradiated by the device. Thus, Peter ends up gaining superpowers linked to the animal.
